Slow Cooker Mac and Cheese

Description

This recipe is for macaroni and cheese made in a slow cooker or crock pot. It takes approximately 3 hours and 15 minutes to cook with a prep time of about 15 minutes. This recipe is sized to make 12 servings.

Alternatively this can be cooked in a casserole dish in a conventional oven (set to 350 degrees and bake for 45 minutes to one hour) for a faster cook time.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) package elbow macaroni
  • 1/2 cup butter
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1 (16 ounce) package shredded cheddar cheese, divided
  • 1 (5 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • 2 eggs, well beaten
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 (10.5 ounce) can condensed cheddar cheese soup (such as Campbell's)
  • 1 pinch paprika, or more to taste (Optional)

Directions

  1. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to rolling boil.
  2. Stir in macaroni and return to a boil.
  3. Cook pasta uncovered, stirring occasionally, until tenter yet firm to the bite (about 8 minutes).
  4. Drain and transfer pasta to a slow cooker.
  5. Add butter to pasta and stir until melted; season with salt and pepper.
  6. Sprinkle about half of the 16 ounces of shredded cheddar cheese over pasta and stir.
  7. Whisk evaporate milk and eggs together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.
  8. Whisk milk and condensed soup together in a bowl until smooth; stir into pasta mixture.
  9. Sprinkle remaining shredded cheese over pasta mixture; garnish with paprika.
  10. Cook on Low for 3 hours.
  11. Serve hot and enjoy!
